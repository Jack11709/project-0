Instructions

Welcome to Garys of Thunder! Race your Gary across the screen to claim victory, each turn you can move your Gary, boost your Gary, or salt your opponent. Boost will give you a guarenteed big move, and salt with prevent your opponents next turn! Be wary, your salt and boost only work once so use them wisely! Play 2 player mode with a friend or take on Mecha Gary in 1 Player!
